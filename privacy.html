TikTok Daily Ops - Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-17

This Privacy Policy explains how the TikTok Daily Ops application (the "Service") handles information when you connect TikTok accounts and use the Service.

1. Information We Collect

  • TikTok user identifiers (e.g., open_id) and profile details provided by TikTok.
  • Video metadata (e.g., title, counts, and share links) provided by TikTok APIs.
  • OAuth tokens required to access TikTok APIs on your behalf.

2. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Sync video metadata from TikTok.
  • Display and manage your videos in the Service.
  • Maintain and secure access to TikTok APIs.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We only share data when required to operate the Service or comply with legal obligations.

4. Data Retention

We retain TikTok metadata and tokens only as long as necessary to provide the Service. You may request removal of your data by contacting the Service owner.

5. Security

We use reasonable measures to protect data at rest and in transit. However, no system can be guaranteed 100% secure.

6. Your Choices

You may revoke access in TikTok and request deletion of your data. Revoking access will stop future syncing.

7. Third-Party Services

The Service relies on TikTok APIs. TikTok’s privacy practices are governed by their own policies.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of the Service after updates indicates acceptance of the changes.

9. Contact

For privacy questions, contact the Service owner or administrator.

TikTok Daily Ops is a third-party tool and is not endorsed by TikTok.
